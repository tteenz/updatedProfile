About Me

Graduated from UC Berkeley Boot Camp program.

I realize my passion for technology when my laptop fell off my bed and the screen cracked. Instead of taking it in to get it fixed, I opened it up and replaced the screen myself. I had fun tinkering around so I started to fix laptop and phone screen on the side. I then got an opportunity to work at Microsoft as a product advisor and enjoy talking about computers. I took a few computer science classe at my local community college before I decided I wanted to enroll in the UC Berkeley program and thus my journey as a Front-End developer began.

Besides being behind a computer screen, I enjoy going on hikes and trying new food. I also like taking pictures and editing them as time permits.
